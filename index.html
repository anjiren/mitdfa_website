Ongoing Project: Bedcount

As part of our design process, we took to the streets with the Street Team from the Harvard Square Homeless Shelter (HSHS) and got to talk to some of the homeless in the area. We found that the homeless often have to call anywhere between 10-30 shelters a day when seeking emergency beds. We are currently working with them to create BedCount, a website for HSHS directors to keep track of their occupancy. In the future, we plan to connect all the shelters in the Boston/Cambridge area through this database to make it easier for the homeless to find shelter.
